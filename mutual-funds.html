Mutual Fund FAQs

What is a Mutual Fund?
A mutual fund is a professionally managed investment fund that pools money from many investors to purchase securities like stocks, bonds, money market instruments, and other assets.
Are Mutual Funds Safe?
Mutual fund investments are subject to market risks. However, they are regulated by SEBI, and professional fund managers work to minimize risks by diversifying the portfolio.
