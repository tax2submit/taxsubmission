Insurance FAQs

How much health insurance do I need?
The ideal health cover depends on your family's medical history, your city of residence, and the number of members to be insured. A minimum of ₹5-10 lakhs is often recommended for a family of four.
What is a Term Plan?
A term plan is a pure life insurance product that offers a large life cover at a very low premium for a fixed period. It pays out a lump sum to your family if you pass away during the policy term.
